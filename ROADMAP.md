# Long-Term Vision

AutoRec aims to become a lightweight attack surface management and continuous reconnaissance platform capable of:

* Asset Discovery
* Vulnerability Discovery
* Change Detection
* Reporting
* Monitoring
* Automation

while remaining simple to deploy, modular, and easy to extend.
